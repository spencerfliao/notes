

Genome ≈ book/recipe ≠ blueprint analogy
- Genes are 1D digital codes, like text (set of letters for variable length of info)
- Blueprints are 2D maps, and are too literal for genetics, because each part of a blueprint makes an equivalent part of the machine or building; each sentence of a recipe book does not make a different mouthful of cake
