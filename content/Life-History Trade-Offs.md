###### Life-History Profile of Humans

| Trait                         | Human Value                | Trade-Off Implication                                                        |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Lifespan**                  | Very long (70–100+ yrs)    | Strong selection for longevity and post-reproductive roles                   |
| **Age at first reproduction** | Late (mid-teens to 30s)    | Delayed reproduction in exchange for long-term investment                    |
| **Parental investment**       | Extremely high             | Few offspring but massive investment per child (food, protection, education) |
| **Fertility rate**            | Low (~1–5 children)        | Focus on offspring _quality_ over quantity                                   |
| **Development time**          | Slow (20+ yrs to maturity) | Long dependency period enables large brain and cultural transmission         |
| **Offspring survival**        | High in modern societies   | Investment in medicine, education, social structures                         |
