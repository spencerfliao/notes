9+1 dimensions
- Violent jittery quantum motion of a strong can pile up
- Only in 9+1D: delicate conditions met so that oscillation of all modes of every string precisely match each other and become undetectable

Supersymmetric moduli space
- Every fermion/boson has its boson/fermion
- Vacuum energy = 0
- Most of string theory research is about this plain

M-theory
- Not many theories, but many solutions
- Correspond to extreme values of the moduli (corner of the landscape)
- Continuously vary so that one morphs into any other
