
Definitions
happiness 幸福
- comfortable pleasant
- 幸福不是某种状态，而是一种能力 (既先天上有区别，又可以培养)
meaningful 意义
- fulfilling, virtuous, sense of purpose, involves devotion
psychologically rich 丰富性
- eventful, dramatic, interesting, full of surprise
- 挑战性，也许是艰难的
- 二手经历也可以提升体验的丰富性
