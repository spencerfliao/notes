
- cerebrum 大脑
	- cerebral cortex 皮层 (2 hemispheres)
		- frontal lobe 额叶
			- frontal cortex
				- PFC 前额叶皮层
				- premotor cortex 前运动皮层
				- motor cortex 运动皮层
			- basal ganglia 基底核
		- parietal lobe 顶叶: sensory
		- temporal lobe 颞叶: hearing, learning, visual memory, language
		- occipital lobe 枕叶: visual
	- limbic system 边缘系统
		- hippocampus 海马体
		- amygdala 杏仁体
		- cingulum 扣带回
	- ventricles 脑室: cerebrospinal fluid
	- corpus callosum 胼胝体
- cerebellum 小脑
- brainstem 脑干
	- thalamus 丘脑: relay stimuli
	- hypothalamus 下丘脑: autonomic nervous system, homeostatic regulation, links nervous and endocrine systems

###### PFC
![[image-2.png|658x658]]
- PFC 前额皮层 (prefrontal cortex)
	- vmPFC 腹内侧: emotion, empathy, moral (**agranular**)
	- vlPFC 腹外侧: emotion, semantics, inhibition, attention
	- dmPFC 背内侧: social interaction, moral
	- dlPFC 背外侧: planning, working memory, reasoning
	- ACC 前扣带皮层 (anterior cingulate cortex): error detection, conflict monitoring, uncertainty (**agranular**)
	- OFC 眼眶额皮层: taste, smell, impulse control, evaluate reinforcers, adjust behavior for reward
