
a self-defeating loop occurs when a person adopts a strategy to achieve a goal, but the very nature of the **strategy ensures the goal remains unreachable**.

examples:
- chasing happiness
- perfectionism: [[The Gifts of Imperfection Notes#^aba971]]
- seeking approval: [[The Gifts of Imperfection Notes#^15389f]]
- positive thinking to produce better performance: [[The Inner Game of Tennis#^66c016]]
- controlling anxiety
