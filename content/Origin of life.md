
[[Cosmic Origin of Life]]

* Primordial atmosphere + ocean + heat + lightning = amino acids
* Autocatalysis: enzyme that catalyzes the synthesis of itself
* Origin of hereditary: replication + variation = competition = selection
* Protein: any 3d shape by chemical affinities of amino acids - good at being enzymes
* DNA: double helix - good at preserving sequence, being copied, and error proofing
* RNA: lack self-pairing of complementary chain - pair with bits of itself - versatile mix of both
	* 5S ribosomal RNA: smallest and simplest RNA - possibly the origin of life
	* in all domains of life (bacteria, archaea, and eukaryotes, discarded by animal mitochondria)
	* the ingredients of DNA are made by modifying the ingredients of RNA (T from U), not by a more direct route
	* most basic/primitive functions in cells require RNA
	* many protein enzymes rely on small RNAs to make them work
	* only RNA can copy itself without assistance
